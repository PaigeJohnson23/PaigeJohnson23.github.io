About me

My name is Paige Johnson, and I am studying Interior Architecture and Design at Bob Jones University. I am graduating in 2026, and plan to be a licensed residential interior designer.

I have loved painting and drawing ever since I could hold a pencil. I have always had a love for color, and it is one of my favorite things to study today. I have always enjoyed the challenge of painting or drawing nature and capturing its beauty.

All of my work stems from my original photos, or just my imagination! I plan to sell prints, greeting cards, and bookmarks starting in January 2024.
